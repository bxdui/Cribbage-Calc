Adding runs to the Cribbage Calc
--------------------------------
4

IF three cards exist in ascending order (increments of 1), add 3 to the score

IF four cards exist in ascending order (increments of 1), add 4 to the score

IF all five cards exist in ascending order (inrements of 1), add 5 to the score

DOUBLE RUNS:

IF two separate instances of runs exist, count each independently and add their respective scores to the score
